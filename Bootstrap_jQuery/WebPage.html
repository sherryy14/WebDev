About

What defines "cool?" When talking about cars, there are a few key identifiable characteristics that come to mind. A cool car needs to look good above all else – not just its exterior, but in the cabin as well. A cool car needs to perform well, offer the latest and greatest technology, and in some cases, come in at an affordable price. The term “cool” doesn't just apply to six-figure supercars (though, you'll find some here). With that in mind, we've compiled a list of the 25 coolest vehicles you can buy in 2020 and 2021 – and it's not just sports cars. There are a number of cool sedans, crossovers, and even pickup trucks on sale today or upcoming that will definitely turn heads while on the road. If you're in the market for something cool, use this as your definitive guide.
